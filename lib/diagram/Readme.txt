Unpack .zip archive wherever you want.  Lets call that place PATH.
To install and correctly show lstree.html and lsh_safe.html web pages you need to:

1). Install Google Chrome and use it for those files.
2). Open Google Chrome and go to the right upper corner of the screen. Click 'Menu Button' with 'three bricks' icon.
3). Choose 'Tools->Extensions'.
4). Set 'Developer Mode' flag (on the right side of 'Extensions' page title).
5). Click 'Load unpacked extension' and choose  PATH\learnsmart folder.

Done!
